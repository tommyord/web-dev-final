Featured Video

"Grit: The Power of Passion and Perseverance" — Angela Lee Duckworth

Why I picked this

In this TED talk, psychologist Angela Lee Duckworth makes the case that the best predictor of success isn't IQ, talent, or social intelligence — it's grit. She defines grit as passion and perseverance for long-term goals, and the stamina to stick with your future day in, day out.

I picked this video because the message hits home. Whether it's working toward a career in finance, serving a two-year mission abroad, or starting a service club from scratch, the results I'm most proud of have all come from sticking with something long after it stopped being easy. Duckworth's research gives language to something I've seen play out in my own life.
